    PROJECTS

    MergeMapp:
    A travel app case study
    Problem:

    With the recent lifting of Covid restrictions, young adults have begun travelling with their friends again however, planning trips for groups is a stressful and time consuming process.

    Solution:

    I developed MergeMapp, a collaborative planning app that allows travellers to share maps with travel buddies. Every member of the group can add pins as well as share locations and communicate using the chat feature.

    Canada Council for the Arts:
    A government agency website redesign
    Problem:

    The Canada Council of the Arts’ provides Canadian artists and donors with the necessary information to apply for grants and prizes, and donate to the cause. However, due to the current information architecture and UI design, users are having trouble navigating the site and finding necessary information.

    Solution:

    I redesigned and restructured the website using multiple rounds of usability testing and card sorting to improve the website’s information architecture, overall visual design, and responsiveness with mobile browsers.

    Christian Counselling Services:
    A non-profit organization website redesign
    Problem:

    The website was designed to provide potential clients and donors with the necessary information for submitting a counselling request and donating but its poor UI and outdated appearance may cause potential clients and donors to look elsewhere.

    Solution:

    By redesigning the CCS website for low-earning individuals and donors we will not only increase the amount of counselling requests they receive but also increase the amount of donations.
